About Us

The RIT Delivery Service aims to a provide fast, reliable and safe on-campus food delivery option for RIT students.

Our mission is to offer convenient access to campus dining for busy students, reducing wait times and enabling efficient meal ordering.


Our target audience is full-time on-campus students, particularly those balancing coursework, campus activities, and part-time work

who prioritize quick and easy meal access.


The RIT Delivery Service is an official third party of RIT.
